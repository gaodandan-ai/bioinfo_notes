    "# Growth Curve Features Documentation\n",

    "## 1. Model fit and quality control (模型拟合与质量控制)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features related to the goodness of fit of the Gompertz model and data quality.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **AIC / BIC** | Model information criteria (信息准则) | Akaike Information Criterion (AIC) and Bayesian Information Criterion (BIC) for the Gompertz model fit. Lower values indicate a better trade-off between fit quality and model complexity. |\n",
    "| **fit_RMSE** | Gompertz fit error (拟合均方根误差) | Root Mean Square Error between the raw OD data and the fitted Gompertz model curve. Measures how well the model describes the data. |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 2. Integrated growth output (积分生长总量)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features representing the total biomass accumulation over time (Area Under the Curve).\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **AUC** | Total Area Under the Curve (总曲线下面积) | $\\int_{0}^{T} OD(t) dt$. Represents the total biomass exposure over the entire experiment duration. |\n",
    "| **AUC_early** | Early biomass accumulation (早期AUC) | Area under the curve during the first 1/3 of the experiment time. |\n",
    "| **AUC_mid** | Mid-phase biomass accumulation (中期AUC) | Area under the curve during the middle 1/3 of the experiment time. |\n",
    "| **AUC_late** | Late biomass accumulation (晚期AUC) | Area under the curve during the last 1/3 of the experiment time. |\n",
    "| **back_loaded_fraction** | Late growth dominance (晚期占比) | `AUC_late` / `AUC`. High values indicate growth occurred mostly towards the end. |\n",
    "| **front_loaded_fraction** | Early growth dominance (早期占比) | `AUC_early` / `AUC`. High values indicate rapid early growth. |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 3. Decline and survival (衰退与存活)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features characterizing the death phase, lysis, or biomass loss after the peak.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **decline_fraction** | Extent of biomass loss (衰退比例) | $\\frac{\\text{max\\_OD} - \\text{Endpoint\\_biomass}}{\\text{max\\_OD}}$. Represents the proportion of peak biomass lost by the end of the experiment. |\n",
    "| **decline_onset_time** | Start of decline (衰退开始时间) | The time point after `t_peak` where the specific growth rate $\\mu(t)$ consistently becomes negative. |\n",
    "| **half_life_after_peak** | Biomass half-life (峰后半衰期) | Time required for OD to drop to 50% of `max_OD` after reaching the peak. |\n",
    "| **late_OD_slope** | Late phase slope (晚期斜率) | Linear slope of OD vs. Time calculated over the final 15% of the experiment duration. Negative values indicate death/lysis. |\n",
    "| **max_decline_rate** | Fastest death rate (最大衰退速率) | The minimum (most negative) specific growth rate $\\mu(t)$ observed during the decline phase. |\n",
    "| **persistence_time** | Time spent at high biomass (高生物量维持时间) | The total duration for which $OD(t) \\ge 0.7 \\times \\text{max\\_OD}$. |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 4. Multi-phase / Diauxic growth (多阶段/双相生长)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features detecting complex growth patterns like diauxie (two distinct growth phases).\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **diauxic_lag** | Diauxic lag duration (双相生长停滞期) | Time difference between the first and second peaks in the specific growth rate $\\mu(t)$. |\n",
    "| **n_phases** | Number of growth phases (生长阶段数) | The number of distinct local maxima detected in the $\\mu(t)$ curve. |\n",
    "| **second_mu_max** | Secondary growth rate (第二最大速率) | The magnitude of the second highest peak in the specific growth rate $\\mu(t)$ curve (if present). |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 5. Biomass yield and carrying capacity (生物量产量与承载力)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features measuring the maximum amount of biomass achieved.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **Endpoint_biomass** | Final biomass (终点生物量) | The OD value at the very last time point of the experiment. |\n",
    "| **max_OD** | Maximum biomass achieved (最大生物量) | The absolute maximum OD value recorded during the experiment ($\\max OD(t)$). |\n",
    "| **model_A (asymptote)** | Carrying capacity (理论承载量) | The asymptote parameter ($A + y_0$) derived from the Gompertz model fit ($y(t) = A e^{-e^{B-Ct}} + y_0$). |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 6. Exponential phase properties (指数期特性)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features specific to the main exponential growth phase.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **exp_fit_R2** | Exponential fit R² (指数期拟合优度) | $R^2$ of the linear regression of $\\ln(OD)$ vs time during the identified exponential phase. |\n",
    "| **exp_OD_gain** | Biomass gained during exponential phase (指数期增量) | The difference in OD between the end and start of the exponential phase ($OD_{end} - OD_{start}$). |\n",
    "| **Exp_phase_length** | Duration of exponential growth (指数期时长) | The time duration of the exponential phase (`t_exp_end` - `t_exp_start`). |\n",
    "| **mean_mu** | Average specific growth rate (平均比生长速率) | The average value of $\\mu(t)$ during the valid growth phase. |\n",
    "| **t_exp_end** | End of exponential phase (指数期结束时间) | The time point marking the end of the maximal growth rate period. |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 7. Growth curve shape and dynamics (曲线形状与动态)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features describing the geometric shape and acceleration/deceleration of the curve.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **growth_timing_asymmetry**| Growth symmetry (生长不对称性) | $\\frac{t_{90} - t_{50}}{t_{50} - t_{10}}$. Measures if growth is skewed towards the beginning or end. Value > 1 implies slower late growth. |\n",
    "| **max_acceleration** | Max acceleration of growth (最大生长加速度) | The maximum value of the second derivative of OD ($\\max \\frac{d^2OD}{dt^2}$). |\n",
    "| **max_deceleration** | Max deceleration of growth (最大生长减速度) | The minimum value of the second derivative of OD ($\\min \\frac{d^2OD}{dt^2}$), usually at the end of the log phase. |\n",
    "| **model_shape (ν, etc.)** | Gompertz shape parameter (模型形状参数) | Parameters from the Gompertz model ($B$ or $C$) that define the sigmoid curve's steepness and inflection. |\n",
    "| **t_inflection** | Inflection point (拐点时间) | The time point where the growth acceleration changes from positive to negative (max growth rate). |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 8. Intrinsic growth capacity and kinetics (内生生长能力与动力学)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features describing the fundamental speed of growth.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **initial_mu (μ₀)** | Initial growth rate (初始生长速率) | The average specific growth rate calculated immediately after the detection time ($t_{detect}$ to $t_{detect}+2h$). |\n",
    "| **max_dODdt** | Maximum absolute growth rate (最大绝对增长率) | The maximum slope of the raw OD curve ($\\max \\frac{dOD}{dt}$). |\n",
    "| **max_mu** | Maximum specific growth rate (最大比生长速率) | The maximum value of the specific growth rate $\\mu(t) = \\frac{d\\ln(OD)}{dt}$. |\n",
    "| **mean_mu** | Average specific growth rate (平均比生长速率) | (Also listed in Exp. Phase) The mean of valid $\\mu(t)$ values. |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 9. Lag and growth inhibition (滞后期与生长抑制)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features measuring the delay before growth starts.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **Lag_cost_index** | Lag cost (滞后代价指数) | `lag_phase` $\\times$ `max_mu`. Quantifies the trade-off/loss in potential growth due to the lag phase. |\n",

    "| **t_detect** | Detection time (检测时间) | The time point where OD first significantly rises above the baseline (mean + 3*SD). |\n",
    "| **t_lag_end** | End of lag phase (滞后期结束时间) | Effectively the same as `t_detect` in this pipeline. |\n",
    "\n",
    "## 10. Growth timing and progression (生长时序与进程)\n",
    "\n",
    "Features marking specific time points in the growth curve.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Feature Name | Biological Meaning | Calculation Method / Formula |\n",
    "| :--- | :--- | :--- |\n",
    "| **OD_centroid_time** | Center of mass of growth (生长重心时间) | $\\frac{\\sum t \\cdot OD(t)}{\\sum OD(t)}$. The weighted average time of biomass presence. |\n",
    "| **OD_time_skewness** | Bias towards early or late growth (生长偏度) | Weighted skewness of the time vector, weighted by biomass. |\n",
    "| **t10** | Time to 10% growth (到达10%生长量的时间) | Time when OD reaches 10% of the total net growth ($OD_0 + 0.1 \\Delta OD$). |\n",
    "| **t50** | Time to 50% growth (到达50%生长量的时间) | Time when OD reaches 50% of the total net growth. |\n",
    "| **t90** | Time to 90% growth (到达90%生长量的时间) | Time when OD reaches 90% of the total net growth. |\n",
    "| **t_AUC50** | Time to 50% accumulated biomass (半AUC时间) | The time $t$ at which the cumulative area under the curve reaches 50% of the total AUC. |\n",
    "| **t_peak_from_start** | Time to peak relative to start (相对峰值时间) | `time_max_OD` - `t_start`. |\n",
    "| **time_max_mu** | Time of fastest growth (达到最大速率的时间) | The time point where `max_mu` occurs. |\n",
    "| **time_max_OD / t_peak** | Time to peak biomass (达到峰值的时间) | The time point where the maximum OD is observed. |\n",
